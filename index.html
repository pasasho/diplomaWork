Where do you want your adventure to take you?

Roll your dice!

Here you can roll from 4 to 20 sided dies. Just like the real thing. No more rolling mostly 1s!

When you click on any die, it will generate a number for it.

Make your character!

A simplified way to make a character. Only the essentials for new players.

You can select a level, ability stats and proficiencies of your character. Along with Armour Class (AC) and equipped armour, and you can track the Health Points(HP) and damage dealt to the character. There is a save and load option to use the character sheet again.

If you find this too complicated, you can just click on Generate Character!

Choose your Spells!

A simple list for a quick look at all the official spells for 5e!

You can click on any spell and it's description will pop up. There is a search bar as well, which lets you search for spells by typing in a Spell Name or the Class of your character!

Generate a person for your fantasy world!

Create a person with a backstory and personality details, using this generator!.

Useful for Dungeon Masters to create non-player characters instantly! Fill any of the fields, if you want, and click Generate and it will fill the rest of the fields!

Resources to help along the way!

A helpful collection of printable dice and maps, as well as a collection of useful links for any D&D player!

Click on any of the cards and they will flip and reveal their respective resources.
